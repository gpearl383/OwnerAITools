Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how CSM Integrated Solutions LLC collects, uses, and protects personal information in connection with the OwnerAI Tools website (owneraitools.com), our phone lines, and the AI receptionist services we provide ("Services").

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide directly: name, business name, phone number, email address, mailing address, and any details you share when you contact us by phone, email, web form, or when you book a call with us.
  • Call information: when you call our phone lines (including our demo line) or when our AI receptionist answers calls on behalf of a client business, calls may be recorded and transcribed. We collect the caller's phone number, the contents of the conversation, recordings, transcripts, and automated summaries.
  • SMS information: if you opt in to text messages, we collect your mobile number and your message history with us.
  • Technical information: standard website logs such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring pages, used for site operation and analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Services, including answering calls, capturing messages, scheduling appointments, and delivering call summaries to our clients.
  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule requested calls or demos.
  • To send service-related communications, including SMS messages you have opted in to receive.
  • To train and improve the accuracy of the specific AI receptionist configurations we operate. We do not sell your data or use it to train third-party public models.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

3. Call Recording Notice

Calls to and from our phone lines, including calls handled by our AI receptionists, may be recorded and transcribed for quality assurance, service delivery, and record-keeping. Where required by law, callers are notified and/or consent is obtained before recording.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the Services (e.g., telephony and SMS carriers, voice AI infrastructure, email delivery, cloud hosting, and scheduling providers), bound by confidentiality obligations and permitted to use the information only to provide services to us.
  • Our clients: when our AI receptionist answers a call on behalf of a client business, the information the caller provides (such as name, number, and reason for calling) is delivered to that business.
  • Legal authorities when required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
SMS consent is never shared or sold. No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All categories of personal information described above exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third parties.

If you opt in to text messages, message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. See our SMS Terms & Conditions for program details, opt-out (STOP), and help (HELP).

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information, call recordings, and transcripts only as long as needed for the purposes described above, to serve our clients, and to meet legal and accounting requirements. You may request deletion of your information at any time using the contact details below, subject to legal retention obligations.

6. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information, including encryption of data in transit and at rest with our infrastructure providers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP (see SMS Terms below).
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@owneraitools.com.
  • Depending on your state of residence, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws. We honor verified requests as required by law.

8. Cookies & Tracking

The owneraitools.com website uses cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising. When you first visit, a consent banner lets you accept or decline these cookies; no analytics or advertising cookies are set unless you accept. You can change your choice at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the footer of any page.

The cookies we use, when you consent, are:

  • Essential: a single first-party cookie that remembers your cookie choice for 12 months. This is set regardless of your choice and contains no personal information.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics: helps us understand how visitors find and use the site (pages viewed, traffic sources, approximate location). If you decline, Google receives only aggregated, cookieless signals that cannot identify you.
  • Advertising — Google Ads: measures whether our ads led to a visit, call, or booking, and may be used for remarketing. Opt out of Google's personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.
  • Advertising — Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Pixel: measures ad performance on Meta platforms and may be used for remarketing. The Pixel loads only if you accept cookies. Manage your Meta ad preferences at facebook.com/adpreferences.

Our hosting provider also collects standard server logs (such as IP address and pages requested) for security and site operation, and our font provider serves font files without setting cookies. Some browsers offer a Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal; where required by applicable law, we honor it as an opt-out of targeted advertising.

9. Children's Privacy

The Services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.
